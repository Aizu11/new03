Welcome To National Institute Of Technical And Health Education Council (N.I.T.H.E.C)

National Institute of Technical and Health Education Council, have been run under The Best Education Society Regd. by Govt. of India New Delhi Regd. an Educational Organization, started Teacher's Training Courses all over India, with the help & Co-operation of educational experts with a view to train unemployed educated girls, particularly the girls of Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribes and Backward class, so that they may impart education, based on modern lines to Pre-school, with a stress on moral, intellectual, physical & social personality, as it was found, that there was no training for teachers in India for Pre-school going children education.
